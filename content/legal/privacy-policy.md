This Privacy Policy explains how we handle visitor data, cookies, and local storage.

## 1. Information We Collect & Use
This is a **static website** that **does not actively collect, store, or upload any personally identifiable information through automated means** (such as cookies or analytics), including but not limited to:
- Name, email address, phone number, physical address
- Account credentials, payment information
- Precise geolocation data

**Exception: Communication Data**
If you choose to contact us via the email address provided on this website, the email address you send from, and any content included in your message, will be stored. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiries or address your requests. We do not use this data for marketing purposes unless you have explicitly consented to receive such communications.

We do not sell, share, or use any data for advertising or marketing purposes unless explicitly stated.

## 2. Local Storage (localStorage)
To improve user experience, this website uses browser **localStorage**:
- Purpose: To **save your theme preferences (dark/light mode, layout settings, etc.)**
- Storage location: **Only on your local browser**
- Data nature: **completely anonymous, no personal data, never uploaded to any server**
- We cannot access, read, or transmit this data

You may delete this information at any time by clearing your browser’s cache or local storage.

## 3. Website Analytics – Umami
This website uses **Umami** for lightweight, privacy-focused analytics:
- Umami collects only **anonymous, aggregated data**, such as page views, traffic sources, session duration, device type, and browser type.
- Umami **does not use cookies**, does not track individuals, and does not collect personal identifiers.
- Data is used solely to improve website content and structure, and is never used for advertising.
- Data processing is governed by Umami’s privacy policy.

## 4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies
- This website **does not use any cookies on its own**.
- When you click on an **Amazon affiliate link**, Amazon may use cookies or similar technologies for:
  - Affiliate commission attribution and tracking
  - Purchase conversion analytics
  - Personalized product recommendations

We **do not control, access, or store any cookie data from Amazon**.
